Google Reader (Previously Starred) 2 pocket (read it later) converter

Google Reader was shut down on July 1 2013. I started to use pocket as an alternative. The problem was that I had saved starred items in Google Reader and wanted to move them to pocket. Pocket had an import tool but not for google readers json takeout.

So I made this, it converts the google reader takout file to instapaper format and that can be imported to reader.

If the file is too large it will crash, I don't have solution, my starred file was small.

  1. Go to your Google "Reader Settings" > click Import/Export > click "Download your data through Takeout" > click "Create Archive" > click "Download" > open the archive and copy the json from the "starred.json" file
